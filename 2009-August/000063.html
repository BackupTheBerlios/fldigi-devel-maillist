Thank you for your inquiry, but all of the fldigi source code is 
licensed under the GPL.  It cannot be relicensed under any other 
licensing agreement.  Please refer to: 
http://www.gnu.org/copyleft/gpl.html.  You are free to use it for 
commercial purposes, but you would then have to comply with the GPL and 
place any modified, improved or derived source for the new product under 
the GPL.  The GPL requires that all other source that comprises the new 
application be GPL as well.  This may seem somewhat Draconian, but the 
intent is to produce open source that can benefit other developers who 
are willing to work within the confines of the GPL.
