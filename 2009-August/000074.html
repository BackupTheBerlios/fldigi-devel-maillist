Stelios Bounanos wrote:
> The search_up and search_down functions are only implemented for PSK and
> RTTY; in other modems they do nothing.  Perhaps some of the other modems
> could get this feature too -- Dave will know :)
>   
Domino and Thor would be good candidates for an automated search.  MFSK 
and Olivia have too much latency in the decoding path to allow a search 
acquisition to work.  They also require very accurate tuning.  MT63, if 
used as intended, only transmits on increments of 500 Hz.  Signal 
acquisition only requires that the decoder be within +/- 100 Hz of the 
actual transmitted center frequency.  Throb might also be a candidate 
for a search algorithm.  Feld is a quasi FAX mode and very visual.  You 
would probably not benefit from trying to implement automatic search for 
any of the Feld modes.
