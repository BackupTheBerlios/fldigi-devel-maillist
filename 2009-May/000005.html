I raise this here for fldigi, though, since I want to ask whether maybe 
it shouldn't be using modal dialog boxes to report occasional, transient 
errors in rig control.

Ideally, every single RS232 command should be received error-free and 
every rig respond correctly.  But my experience has been that they 
occasionally go astray, and I think it ought to take more than an 
occasional, stray failed response to trigger a modal dialog.
