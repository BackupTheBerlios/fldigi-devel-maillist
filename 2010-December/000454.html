Version 3.21.0AV
  Alpha release

RTTY adjacencies
  Corrected logic to suppress adjacent channel tracking on RTTY browser

PSK AFC
  Restored AFC to both PSK and PSK browser channels.

The restored AFC should provide better tracking on all of the psk 
signals and improve the correlation between the browser and a selected 
signal on the waterfall.  The presence of duplicate tracking on both the 
PSK and the RTTY browser is a function of noise, signal level, and the 
squelch setting.  If you put the squelch too low for the present band 
conditions you will cause both duplicates and excess channel noise 
detection.
