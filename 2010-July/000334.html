 Hi!

 Using JPEG2000 should improve MFSKpix mode (smaller file size). As an
 addition you may use Part 11 of the standard which adds protection of the
 codestream against transmission errors.

 JPWL info:
 http://www.openjpeg.org/index.php?menu=doc#jpwl

 Possible library:
 http://www.jpeg.org/jpeg2000/j2kpart11.html
