    Macros
    
      * Added CLEARLOG macro tag
        - Inserts a ^C into the transmit text.  ^C can also be entered
          manually.  Note that this is a two character sequence:
          carat C, and not a control-C symbolism.
      * Added CLRTX macro tag
        - Inserts a ^X into the transmit text. Upon execution the entire
          transmit buffer will be cleared.
      * Added CLR2X macro tag
        - Inserts a ^x into the transmit text. Upon execution the transmit
          buffer will be cleared up to and including the current transmit
          cursor position
