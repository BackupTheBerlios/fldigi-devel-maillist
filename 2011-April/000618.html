Wanted to say hello to the fldigi developer group and introduce the modes I have been working on.
A short presentation is available at http://studentweb.stcloudstate.edu/phjo1001/pcmode0.8/PCMODE-0.8.pdf

The modes are called PCMODE and are intended to be Pactor-like in bandwidth and throughput. The latest release, pcmode-0.8-beta, will be moved into my fldigi git branch.


For an up-to-date version of the files necessary to compile and run these modes, please see my student website at: http://studentweb.stcloudstate.edu/phjo1001/

Feel free to email me with any questions about the modes, remembering that I am still an undergraduate and may not have a quick answer.
Feedback from anyone willing to beta-test would be greatly appreciated, as the mode is still in development.
