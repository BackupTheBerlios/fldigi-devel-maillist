John,

I've asked a few ham friends who have been pioneers with digital modes in
current and past years, and there was a bit of a backlash to the use of
"pactor" to compare your new work.  It may be that enough hams have an
emotional response to the word that there will be difficulties getting
your message through the noise (so to speak), so if it's not too late, you
might want to consider de-emphasizing the pactor comparison to a footnote.

Are you doing anything that requires retransmits ("ARQ") or quick turnover
of TX/RX, by the way?  Those hallmarks of Pactor seem to have been one
cause for concern.
