Hi all,

I recently updated from FLRig 1.0.1.1O to 1.1.3 and noticed that this
version is no longer following the manual VFO changes on my TS-2000, went
back to the old version and it is ok, compiled 1.1.2 and it exhibits the
same behavior. Band changes or manual tuning of the vfo not visible and
not followed by FLDigi also noticed that when FLRig comes up and for
example the pre-amp is on, flrig does not follow (button not lit) pressing
pre-amp on the rig (or NB) does not change the status in FLRig. Pressing
the buttons in FLRig do however change the setting on the TS-2000, so do
the frequency changes in FLRig, nicely followed by the TS-2000, just not
the other way around....

I have RTS/CTS set, as well as 2 stopbits (just as in the previous version).

Anyone else seen this and perhaps knows where to look for a fix?

I am running FLDigi 3.21.8, FLRig 1.1.3 on OpenSuse 11.2 64bit.
