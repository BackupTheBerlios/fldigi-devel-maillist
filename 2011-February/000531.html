Well, they say that the proof of the pudding is in the taste.  I saw an 
old friend calling CQ on MT63, 20 meters this afternoon.  I made sure 
that the fixed 500 Hz format would copy his signal, and then purposely 
put him near the center of my transceiver audio passband.

Here are some screen shots that tell it all :-)







He was @ 30 Wts and I was @ 10 Wts.  He was seeing basically the same 
QRM on my signal with 100% print.

I think it is safe to say that my DSP changes to the MT63 modem are 
working iaw design.  The MixW developers must have used a totally 
different approach to the manual tuning solution.  The MixW setup dialog 
for MT63 warns that the manual mode requires considerable more cpu power 
to make things work.  The difference in cpu usage between the fixed and 
manual tune processing is not measurable on my Linux system.

73, Dave, W1HKJ
