> Jack ?  OSS4 <> Pulse <> fldigi ?

Neither. This was a "clean" OSS4 based system without Alsa, Pulse or
Jack installed.

The Debian OSS4 install automatically created the /dev/dsp link to the
relevant OSS4 device. The sound device could be selected, with results
of sorts, via both "OSS" and "Portaudio" in the Fldigi soundcard
config dialog, but one had show-stopping problems.

The biggest challenge was initialising the soundcard correctly. This
involved some semi-random fiddling in ossxmix to get everything to
work, then capturing the soundcard config using "ossmix -c" to dump
the ossmix commands needed to restore that config. That was then
included in my fldigi startup script.

I'm travelling, without access to the machine, at the moment, but will
post more details when I get back in 2 or 3 days. Don't want to risk
giving inaccurate info here from memory.
