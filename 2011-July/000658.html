> Perhaps this question has already been asked.  Have you and other
> programmers for FLDigi considered adding the most common of the military
> digital modes to FLDigi, those military modes that are defined in
> MIL-STD-188-110A and MIL-STD-188-110B?  There is a need to have the
military
> and NATO digital modes added.  The need stems from the increased interest
by
> MARS to interface with US military, Government agencies (Federal Emergency
> Management Agency, Transportation Security Administration, others),
> non-Government agencies (NGO's), and NATO allies.  MARS members have few
> options to gain access to these modes and a program like with FLDigi
> including them would be a huge help.  The technical specifications are
> detailed in these documents (attached also).  Specifically,
>
>
>
> *       In MIL-STD-188-110A, the specification for HF modems begins in
> paragraph 5.3 (pages 22 through 42).
>
> *       In MIL-STD-188-110B, the specification for HF modems begins in
> paragraph 5.3 (pages 28 through 48).
>
>
>
> I hope you will consider adding these two military modes to FLDigi.
