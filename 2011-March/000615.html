Hi Dave,

Apologies for the delay, our entire household managed to get the flu. I have modified the patch you sent me and changed the names of the modes to match the conventions already used within Fldigi.

Summary of the changes:
* Name changed from Pacmodem to PCMODE to alleviate confusion. (Not really a modem, but a mode)
* Modes now categorized by the baud rate rather than the bitrate.
* Modes listed as "R" for robust instead if "F" for forward error correction.

Still on my todo list:
* Find a non-intrusive way to force FEC off when using PCMODE85 and PCMODE125 (DominoEX).
* Is the current Interleaver length optimal for this baud rate? Currently 40 bits in PCMODE125R is 80ms.
* Had a knowledgeable individual recommend adding scrambling/randomization to the modem to increase robustness with little extra overhead... opinions welcome.
