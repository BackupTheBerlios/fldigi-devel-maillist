Hi.
This problem appears in newer versions of libfltk 1.10, as the current
one on debian sid (unstable).
The problem is the gettext call in globals/globals.cpp circa line 115:
   { MODE_ANALYSIS, &anal_modem, "ANALYSIS", _("Freq Analysis"), "",     "", "" }

Remove the gettext call and try rebuilding.

73 de Fer
