The FCC has issued a R&O allowing digital modes on 60 meters, including 
PSK31.
FCC-licensed hams will start using fldigi on 60m, and there are some 
areas where they will need help.

It's probably best to read it yourself for the details, but one issue 
affecting fldigi users is that all digital transmissions will be limited 
to center frequency.    I'm worried about the pan-click and how confused 
US hams are going to be about the restriction to stay on the center 
frequency, especially since the center frequency definition is counter 
to what we see on our VFO normally.

Is there some feature we could consider which would help with this? 
Here's one idea:
1. If callsign is in USA according to CTY.DAT we could offer a popup 
about 60M operations.
The popup could have a "never show this again" button and a "help me 
with 60m" button.
2. The 60m help could assure that the rig VFO control is working and 
restrict the waterfall click to the 1.5 KHz center, and offer the 
channelization support for the new frequencies (once they go into 
effect.)  We're losing one frequency and gaining another.
