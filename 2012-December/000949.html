Lynn, is this just another occurrence of the problem you reported a few
weeks ago?  It is likely that the problem is a bug in fldigi triggered
by something in your $HOME/.fldigi/ configuration directory.  I
recommend the same "reset" procedure that I suggested previously:
https://lists.berlios.de/pipermail/fldigi-devel/2012-November/000937.html


As for installing a newer version of fldigi, that's a good idea also as
the bug may have already been fixed.  On an Ubuntu system, you can
install the latest version from my PPA archive by issuing the three
commands at the top of this page:
http://w1hkj.com/LaunchpadInstall.html
(Synaptic will then show the new version, and will automatically offer
you updates as I upload newer revisions to my PPA).
