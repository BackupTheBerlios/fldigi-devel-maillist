Hello,

I have noticed that with fltk-1.3, there seems to be a text
wrapping issue (with fldigi 3.21.24 in Fedora 16).

Looking at the latest fldigi source code, I cannot understand
how the FTextRX::add() function could be correct.

Specifically, we do not call show_insert_position if the line
got wrapped.

The specific symptom I get corroborates this.  Sometimes I have
to drag the vertical scroll bar in the rx window up and down,
to see the latest letters that were drawn into the rx buffer.

When the cursor reaches the end of the line, it does not always
move to the next line - sometimes it just disappears and any
further characters being written do not show up on the screen
until I move the vertical scroll bar up and down...

Is there a missing call to show_insert_position when we line
wrap, or am I misunderstanding something?
