> fldigi quit working. It will not load. I have removed and reinstalled

Hi Lynn-

As Ed pointed out, its probably a good idea to update to a newer version
of fldigi.  The bug causing it to crash may have been fixed already.
But that said...

For any version of fldigi, if its crashing on startup but the same
version used to work fine for you, you can often resolve the problem by
pushing your ".fldigi" configuration directory aside (rename it to
something else) and starting with a fresh configuration.  Fldigi will
create a fresh new .fldigi directory for you when it starts up and
doesn't find an existing one.   Of course, you'll then need to re-enter
your callsign, rig control setup, macro configuration, etc. -- or
carefully copy those bits from the old .flgidi/ directory to the new.

Example of this procedure:

        kamal at fourier:~$ cd $HOME
        kamal at fourier:~$ mv .fldigi my-maybe-broken-dot-fldigi-dir

... then try starting up fldigi again.

Note also that your saved log files are in .fldigi/logs/...  If you
rename your .flgidi directory, its a good idea to poke around in the old
directory for log files and/or other interesting files that you may have
saved there.
