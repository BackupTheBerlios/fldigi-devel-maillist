Hello all,

Many thanks for the THOR100 real-world broadcast from VOA radiogram :)
Wanted to share the testing I have done on THOR100 using the VOA Radiogram broadcast # 32.

Attached are 2 text files comparing the THOR soft decoder decisions between a Perfect audio file and the recorded Real-world broadcast.
In the REAL.decode.txt file; a minus as the first character on a line represents a decoder error (wrong symbol chosen). The string of numbers printed at the end of a line are symbol numbers caught/blocked by the dynamic CWI detector.

In manually looking through the results, the THOR soft-decoder performs well (makes proper decisions), although the "softdoppler" value may be a bit sensitive.

In the future I will do the same fine-tooth analysis for an MFSK broadcast.
