To be honest, I do not completely understand either.

What I do know is:
* There are a few changes upcoming in newest versions of automake that
break backwards compatibility
* Certain macros have been changed or removed
* Something about these automake changes affected the parsing of "
$(srcdir) " specifically on the " TESTS= " line
* Perhaps this a temporary bug in automake, maybe a persistent change.

Also see :
https://lists.gnu.org/archive/html/automake/2012-12/msg00038.html
