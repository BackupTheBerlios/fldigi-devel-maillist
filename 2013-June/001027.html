I am not sure I understand the issue. As a long time Mac OS X user I had no issues with the hamlib integrated into fldigi when I used the Kenwood TS-570D and TS-570S; however, when I upgraded to the Kenwood TS-590S, the Hamlib option in fldigi no longer worked.  The problem as I recall was the backend needed to be updated and Dave W1HKJ was not able compile the hamlib updates for all three platforms supported by fldigi.  Dave manually hacked hamlib v1.2.12 to hamlib v1.2.12S.  This "Special" version of hamlib worked with the Kenwood TS-590S; however, none of the subsequent hamlib updates have incorporated into fldigi.  While the old special version hamlib 1.2.12S currently used in the fldigi-3.21.72 works with my Kenwood TS-590S, I changed over to RigCAT a long time ago. RigCAT works fine with the Kenwood TS-590S.

It is unfortunate the format of updates to hamlib cannot be compiled for use in fldigi and I understand the decision to remove hamlib in future fldigi updates. 

73 Dick AA5VU
